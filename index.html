Dürer's Drawing Apparatus

An interactive demo of Albrecht Dürer's perspective drawing apparatus based on Underweysung der Messung mit dem Zirckel und Richtscheyt (A Course in the Art of Measurement with a Compass and Ruler, 1525).

Instructions for the Draughtsman

I

Guide the stylus to a point of interest on the object's surface.

II

Click to mark where the taut thread passes through the frame.

III

Sample multiple points to plot the perspectival outline.

IV

Observe how these points create a "sampling" of the object, much like a modern light ray.
