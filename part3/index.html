The Abstracted Synthesis

Implementing a Real-Time Path Tracer.

This Cornell-style scene demonstrates the interaction of light with various physical surfaces through recursive path tracing:

  • Glass Sphere: A dielectric material with a refractive index of 2.4, simulating realistic refraction and caustics.
  • Specular Box: A rotated cuboid with a perfectly reflective surface, capturing the surrounding environment.
  • Area Light: A rectangular light source at the ceiling that produces soft, physically accurate shadows.
  • Lambertian Walls: Diffuse surfaces (Red, Green, and Gray) that contribute to global illumination through color bleeding.
