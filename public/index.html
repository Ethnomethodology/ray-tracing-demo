Ray Tracing Demo

An Interactive Demo of Dürer's Drawing Apparatus

Based on Underweysung der Messung mit dem Zirckel und Richtscheyt (A Course in the Art of Measurement with a Compass and Ruler, 1525).

Instructions for the Draughtsman

I

Guide the stylus to a point of interest on the object's surface.

II

Click to mark where the taut thread passes through the frame.

III

Repeat the above two steps to draw multiple points from the object.

IV

Observe how a perspective drawing of the object emerges like a pointillist depiction of it.
